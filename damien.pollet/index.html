I am assistant professor (Maître de conférences) at the university of Lille 1 (UFR IEEA) since september 2008.

I teach programming and software engineering at Telecom Lille 1, and research on reengineering at Inria within the RMoD project-team.

I’m a co-author of the open-source books Squeak by Example and Pharo by Example.

Curriculum

I received an engineering degree in computer science from Insa in 2001, and obtained my Ph.D in 2005 from the university of Rennes 1, France (details here).
